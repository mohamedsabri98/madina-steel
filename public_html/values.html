Our Values

1. Excellence: By improving the service to our customers and suppliers to build together a long-term partnership.

2. Commitment: Each and every individual, employee and senior manager in Almadina Steel Group shall be responsible for their tasks to meet the general objectives of the Company as well as the special objectives of the subsidiary departments in a clear and specific vision.

3. Safety: We are consistently keen on our employees, following a proactive approach to provide safety and to preserve environment. Moreover, we are always looking forward to find innovative means to improve our safety performance.

4. Professional Integrity: Integrity and impartiality are integral parts of our relationship with all our partners.

5. Social Responsibility: Our society’s safety and welfare are always our first priority. Our organization is fully committed to society’s responsibility by offering development initiatives and programs. In our Group, we unify our efforts to effectively act in the society, by supporting, promoting and training the promising engineers and technicians in our plants.
