Steel Rebar

  • Products
  • Productive Capacity

Al-Madina Steel Group meets the supply orders for steel rebar products with accurate mechanical and chemical properties, according to the Egyptian standards for construction.
1. The sizes of rebar products range between (10 – 12 – 16 – 18 mm) in order to meet the Egyptian and international standards.
2. Almadina Steel Group has the capacity to supply rebar steel products with various mechanical properties and components, according to our Client’s requirements and fulfilling the Egyptian standards for constructions.
3. Each package is defined in terms of (weight) not exceeding 2 tons with 100 kg-tolerance, and in terms of (length) by 12 m with 0-10 ml tolerance (-0: 10 ml).
4. We adhere to the Egyptian and international standards and as guidelines in the manufacture and/or rolling of rebar steel.
5. Colors are used as a coding system to determine the specific sizes of diameters rebar as per the products identification cards, provided with final products. This facilitates the traceability for clients according to the system adopted of 9001 ISO procedures (Product Traceability).

The actual productivity capacity of the rebar plant is 240,000 tons annually. Almadina Steel aims to enhance the productive capacity by 450,000 tons annually for the production lines (line 1- line 2), while maintaining the quality assurance system within the manufacturing process by the following:

1. Almadina Steel adheres to the highest quality of final products (e.g. rebar steel diameters) or semi- finished products (billets) by the quality management in plants.

2. Using measurement equipment and properties testing machines to specify the tensile strength- yield strength and stretching for rebar steel bars, according to the Egyptian standard specifications.
A periodic calibration of the tensile machine is conducted and certified by the competent authorities, according to 9001 ISO.
3. At Almadina Steel, since our approach relies on quality, our group has applied a comprehensive quality management system and professional safety and health system in plants according to ISO 9001: 2015 aiming at the highest levels of quality and control.

4. Maintaining the mechanical specifications and meter wide of steel bars, in order to enhance the bars’ quality and to create a competitive opportunity for the domestic market and exportation.

5. The Marketing& Quality Department in the plant periodically conducts chemical, mechanical and technical tests in the local universities and reputable calibration and measurement centers (e.g. Cairo University- Ain Shams University- National Research Centre) in order to maintain a fixed and distinguished level of quality.

6. A full commitment of the international standard specifications and Egyptian standards of rebar steel manufacture (ES262-2/2015)
